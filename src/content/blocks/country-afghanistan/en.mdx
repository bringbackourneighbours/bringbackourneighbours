Germany is once again cooperating with the Taliban to deport people to Afghanistan.
The government says it only deports serious criminals to Afghanistan. But the government has lied about this before.

Nevertheless, many Afghans are living in Germany with tolerated stay (Duldung), and only very, very few people are actually deported to Afghanistan.
